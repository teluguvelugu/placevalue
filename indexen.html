Diya and Vivaan’s Place Value Parade

R PARVATHI

It was the day before Independence Day! Diya and Vivaan had a big job: collecting the brightest marigold flowers for the school decorations.

"I'll get the sunny yellow ones!" shouted Vivaan. "And I'll find the ones that are deep orange, like the sunset!" said Diya.

Soon, their baskets were full. They poured all the flowers together and counted them. "One, two, three… all the way to thirty-four beautiful flowers!" cheered Vivaan.

"Thirty-four flowers are too many for one big bunch! How will we carry them without squishing them?" Diya worried. Just then, their wise neighbour, Dadi, walked by with a needle and thread.

"Thirty-four flowers are easy to manage if you use place value," Dadi chuckled. "Let’s make special little garlands! Every garland will have ten flowers in it. Each garland will be a Ten."

Vivaan worked quickly. He strung ten flowers to make one garland. Then another. Then three! "We have three perfect garlands of Ten," said Dadi. "And look! We have four loose flowers left over. Those are the Ones."

Dadi wrote the number 34 in the soil. "The '3' in the Tens place tells us we have three garlands of ten, which is 30. The '4' in the Ones place tells us we have four extra ones!"

"Now for a quick game," Dadi announced. "Look at these two numbers: 23 and 32. They use the same digits, but they are very different! What does the '3' mean in each one?"

Vivaan pointed to the '3' in 32. "That looks like a three," he said. "It is!" Dadi agreed. "That is its Face Value. The face value of a digit is just what it looks like, no matter where it is."

Then Diya pointed to the '3' in 23. "This three is also a three," she said. "Yes, but this time it's in the Ones place!" Dadi explained. "So its Place Value is 3 Ones, or just 3."

"The '3' in 32 is in the Tens place, so its Place Value is 3 Tens, or 30! The place changes the value!" Dadi explained. Diya and Vivaan finally understood. The digits wear different hats depending on their place!

With their Place Value knowledge helping them, carrying 34 flowers was easy. Diya and Vivaan skipped all the way to school, ready to decorate with their beautiful garlands!
